### 1. Conflict Resolution (CLOE vs. DIANA)
*   **The Conflict:** CLOE recommends using literal rate tables directly in the instructions for precision. DIANA warns this is a high-risk maintenance liability due to the volatility of Brazilian tax law (e.g., the near-miss of MP 1.303/2025), which could lead to hallucinated or legally inaccurate net-return calculations if laws change mid-year.
*   **The Resolution: Parameterized Tax Injection.** We will decouple the *tax logic* from the *literal tax values*. The static prompt instructions will define the strict mathematical derivation (how and when to apply IOF, IR, Come-Cotas), but the exact percentages will be passed dynamically as context variables (e.g., `{{TAX_RATES_JSON}}`) at runtime. 
    *   *Why this works:* It prevents the LLM from hallucinating outdated data or assuming MP 1.303 passed, satisfying CLOE’s need for strict brackets while satisfying DIANA’s requirement for maintainability and risk mitigation.

### 2. Final Checklist for Tax Processing
Before finalizing any net-yield calculation, the system must validate the following parameters against the asset profile:
- [ ] **Holding Period Verification:** Calculate the exact holding duration in days (critical for both IOF and Regressive IR).
- [ ] **IOF Trigger:** Is the holding period less than 30 days? (If yes, calculate IOF *before* IR).
- [ ] **Asset Class Exemption Check:** Is the asset an LCI, LCA, CRI, CRA, or Debênture Incentivada held by a Pessoa Física (Individual)? (If yes, apply 100% IR exemption on yield).
- [ ] **FII / Fiagro Differentiation:** Separate dividends (currently exempt for PF) from capital gains on quota sales (flat 20% IR).
- [ ] **Come-Cotas Flag:** Is the asset an open-end investment fund (excluding stock funds/FIIs)? (If yes, model the May/November semiannual anticipated IR deduction).
- [ ] **Tax Base Validation:** Ensure IR and IOF are calculated strictly on the *yield/profit*, never on the principal.

### 4. Ordering of Execution
To ensure mathematical and legal accuracy, the LLM must execute the tax cascade in this strict chronological order:

1.  **Gross Yield Calculation:** Calculate total accrued profit (Gross Principal - Initial Principal).
2.  **Exemption Gate:** If asset is PF-exempt (LCI/LCA/CRI/CRA/Dividends), skip to Step 6.
3.  **IOF Phase:** If Holding Period < 30 days, apply IOF to Gross Yield. Calculate `Yield_After_IOF`.
4.  **Come-Cotas Phase (If Applicable):** Deduct May/Nov anticipations from the yield if the asset is an open-end fund.
5.  **IR Phase:** Apply the correct IR bracket (based on total days held) to the `Yield_After_IOF` (or remaining yield after Come-Cotas). 
6.  **Net Yield Calculation:** Principal + Final Profit (after all deductions).
